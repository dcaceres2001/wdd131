Site Plan

Domain Name: mayansummit.com

The Mayan Summit website is designed to offer tours through Guatemalan Volcanoes, with different options and difficulty levels. The site will feature a clean and modern design, utilizing a color scheme inspired by the natural beauty of the region. The primary colors will be warm tones such as orange and red, complemented by neutral shades for readability.

Scenarios:

What is the tallest volcano in Guatemala?

How can I book a tour to climb a volcano in Guatemala?

What are the different difficulty levels for volcano tours in Guatemala?

Each tour page will provide detailed information about the itinerary, difficulty level, and what to expect. There will also be a booking form for users to reserve their spot on a tour. Additionally, the site will include a blog section where users can read about past tours, tips for hiking volcanoes, and other related content.

Overall, the Mayan Summit website aims to create an engaging and user-friendly experience for adventure seekers looking to explore the beautiful volcanoes of Guatemala.
